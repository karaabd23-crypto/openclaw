# Writing Voice

## Tone preferences

- Direct, technical, concise.
- Operational and action-first.
- American English.

## What to avoid

- Fluff.
- Generic claims without evidence.
- Long prose when bullets are sufficient.

## Sales/copy preferences

- Emphasize concrete outcomes: self-hosted, secure defaults, multi-channel operation.
- Prefer setup/action language over hype.
- Pricing/sales specifics: Needs verification.

## Style preferences for teaching/content/business writing

- Short sections with clear headings.
- Bullet-first structure.
- Include concrete commands/paths when relevant.
- Use checklist/runbook patterns.
